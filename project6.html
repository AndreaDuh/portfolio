The Project

Comic Con is quite the spectacle and once upon a time back in 2014, the higher ups of Comic Con made an app that gave users the ability to view show times and prepare for those shows. Unfortunately, that app was a buggy and ugly mess. With the inclusion of a partner, we designed a new app with a layout that was clean and way more user friendly than the original. It also included things such as alerts and the ability to favorite shows. As well as provide suggestions for things to do in San Diego for tourists. I included this project because it was a fun passion project for me. It showcases my design skills further and it motivated me to take a journey into coding.
