The Project

The goal for this client was to design and code a modern and simple layout utilizing more of the colors in the logo. The client requested more of a visual impact as the past website was bare and needed more design to spruce up the content.

Total Business Network is a platform where businesses can utilize a simple and easier way to advertise their shops and to have a better online presence. The product allows users to search a specific shop catered to their needs. Our target audience for this project were entrepreneurs who were looking to boost their media presence online. This project is currently in the beta stages.
